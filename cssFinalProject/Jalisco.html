Jalisco

Jalisco is one of the most iconic state in Mexico. It's known for dances like 'Son de la Negra' and mariachi music style

The dresses are colorful and the skirts are big enough to form circles around the dancer and the guys costume is called 'charro'.

Jalisco's style is shown in most tourist places and can be easily recognized
