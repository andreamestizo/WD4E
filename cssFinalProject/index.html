Regional Mexican Dances

Mexico has a great cultural variety that is often visible thorugh its folklore.

Some things that caracterize their dances are:

  • The costumes are often colorful
  • Music with vihuela, guitar, trumpet and violin
  • Genres like mariachi, corridos and huapangos

Its important to know that each state has diferent dances influenced by their culture

Jalisco and Nuevo León are the most popular states, if you want to learn more about their dances click on the other pages.
